The Orc and the Pie

You enter a room. In the center of the room is a table with a delicious looking pie on it. An orc looks like he's about to eat the pie. What do you do?


About

I believe it was Monte Cook who originally wrote this adventure as the shortest, yet technically complete, Dungeons and Dragons adventure, including a hook (there's a pie), a dungeon (a 10' x 10' featureless room), a monster (the orc), and treasure (the pie). I first read about it in an article written by Will Wheaton about teaching his step-son to play D&D. Here's my interpretation.
